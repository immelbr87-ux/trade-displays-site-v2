FAQ

Quick answers for buyers and showrooms. This is a pilot-friendly flow designed to feel operational and brand-safe.

Browse displays Example details Request pilot access

Who can buy?

Trade professionals only (contractors, builders, remodelers, designers sourcing installs, property managers). This is not a consumer marketplace.

What am I buying?

Showroom displays scheduled for removal. Items may show cosmetic wear. Listings disclose condition grade (A or B) and key specs.

How does “Reserve” work?

You reserve a display for a specific pickup window at a fixed price. The showroom holds the item for that window once the reservation is confirmed.

When does money move?

Funds are held until pickup confirmation. Once pickup is confirmed (QR + staff confirmation), funds are released to the showroom.

What happens at pickup?

You inspect the item at pickup. The showroom confirms the condition grade on-site. If it matches, the reservation closes and payment is released.

What if the condition doesn’t match?

Pilot behavior:

  • Material mismatches require photo evidence at pickup.
  • If the item materially differs from the listed grade, the reservation can be canceled under the mismatch policy.

Are there returns?

No. Sales are final. The system is designed around inspection + confirmation at pickup.
