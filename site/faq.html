FAQ

Who can buy?

Trade professionals only (contractors, builders, remodelers, designers sourcing installs, property managers). This is not a consumer marketplace.

What am I buying?

Showroom displays scheduled for removal. Items may show cosmetic wear. All listings disclose the condition grade (A or B) and key specs.

How does “Reserve” work?

You reserve a display for a specific pickup window at a fixed price. The showroom holds the item for that window once the reservation is confirmed.

When does money move?

Funds are held until pickup confirmation. Once pickup is confirmed (QR + staff confirmation), funds are released to the showroom.

What happens at pickup?

You inspect the item at pickup. The showroom confirms the condition grade on-site. If the grade matches, the reservation closes and payment is released.

What if the condition doesn’t match?

Condition mismatches require photo evidence at pickup. If the item materially differs from the listed grade, the reservation can be canceled under the mismatch policy.

Are there returns?

No. Sales are final. The system is designed around inspection + confirmation at pickup.

Still unsure? Start by browsing upcoming removals. You’ll see how pickup windows, grades, and pricing are presented before you ever reserve.
