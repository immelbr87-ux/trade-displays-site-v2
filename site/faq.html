Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Trade Displays.

What is Trade Displays?

Trade Displays is a dedicated marketplace for buying, selling, and renting trade show displays. We connect exhibitors with verified sellers, providing a secure platform for transactions with transparent pricing and professional support.

How does Trade Displays work?

Buyers browse our marketplace, select displays, and complete secure transactions. Sellers list their inventory with photos and specifications. We handle payment processing, buyer protection, and provide support throughout the entire process.

Is Trade Displays free to use?

Browsing and buying is completely free for buyers. Sellers pay a small commission (5%) only on completed sales. Our pilot program offers 0% commission for the first 6 months.

Are all sellers verified?

Yes! Every seller goes through a verification process where we check business credentials, contact information, and references. We only approve legitimate businesses with proven track records.

What if I have a problem with my order?

Contact our support team immediately at hello@tradedisplays.com or call (123) 456-7890. We offer dispute resolution services and work to ensure fair outcomes for both parties.

How do I contact customer support?

Email us at hello@tradedisplays.com, call (123) 456-7890 during business hours (M-F, 9am-6pm EST), or use our contact form. We typically respond within 24 hours.

How do I purchase a display?

Browse displays, select one you like, and click "Make Offer" or accept the listed price. Complete the checkout process with your payment and shipping information. The display ships once payment is confirmed.

Are payments secure?

Yes! All payments are processed through our secure escrow system. Your money is held until you confirm receipt of the display. Sellers only receive payment after successful delivery.

What if the display isn't as described?

You have 7 days to inspect the display upon delivery. If it doesn't match the description, contact us immediately. We'll arrange a return and full refund through our buyer protection program.

Can I negotiate the price?

Yes! Most sellers accept offers. Click "Make Offer" on any listing to submit your proposal. Sellers can accept, decline, or counter your offer.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary based on display size, weight, and destination. You'll see the exact shipping cost during checkout before completing your purchase.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we only ship within the United States. International shipping is planned for 2025.

How does rental pricing work?

Rentals are priced per week. We offer discounts for longer durations: 10% off for 2 weeks, 15% off for 1 month. The price shown includes delivery to and return shipping from your event.

When will the display arrive?

Displays typically ship 3-5 days before your event start date to ensure timely arrival. You can specify your preferred delivery date during checkout.

What happens if the display is damaged during my event?

Normal wear and tear is included. For significant damage, you're responsible for repair costs. We offer optional damage waiver insurance for complete peace of mind.

How do I return the display?

Return shipping is included and pre-arranged. We'll send you a prepaid shipping label and pickup instructions. The display must be returned within 24 hours of your event end date.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes! Contact us before your rental period ends to extend. Additional weeks are charged at the standard weekly rate, subject to availability.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your event start date. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit 50% of the rental fee.

How do I become a seller?

Apply through our pilot program page. We'll verify your business credentials and typically approve applications within 24-48 hours.

What are the seller fees?

Standard sellers pay 5% commission on completed sales. Pilot program members (first 100 sellers) pay 0% commission for their first 6 months.

How do I get paid?

Payment is released to your account 2-3 business days after the buyer confirms receipt. We use secure bank transfers for all seller payouts.

Who handles shipping?

Sellers are responsible for packaging and shipping displays to buyers. We provide shipping guidelines and can recommend trusted freight partners for large displays.

Can I list the same display for rent and sale?

Absolutely! Dual listing maximizes your revenue opportunities. You can offer the same display for both rental and purchase with different pricing.

What if a buyer doesn't pay?

Payment is collected upfront through our escrow system. Buyers cannot reserve displays without completing payment, so this isn't an issue.
