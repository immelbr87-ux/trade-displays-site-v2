Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SHOWROOM MARKET.

What is SHOWROOM MARKET?

SHOWROOM MARKET is a B2B marketplace connecting showroom managers with verified trade professionals. We facilitate the sale of premium kitchen and bath floor models, including cabinetry, vanities, appliances, and fixtures at wholesale prices.

How does SHOWROOM MARKET work?

Showrooms list their floor models with photos and details. Trade buyers browse, reserve items with escrow-protected payments, schedule on-site inspections, and arrange pickup. All transactions are pickup-only at the showroom location.

Is SHOWROOM MARKET free to use?

Browsing and buying is completely free for trade buyers. Showrooms pay a small commission (2.5%) only on completed sales. Our 30-day pilot program offers 0% commission for new showrooms.

Are all showrooms verified?

Yes! Every showroom goes through a verification process where we check business credentials, contact information, and references. We only approve legitimate businesses with proven track records.

What if I have a problem with my order?

Contact our support team immediately via our contact form or email. We offer dispute resolution services and work to ensure fair outcomes for both parties with our escrow protection system.

How do I contact customer support?

Use our contact form or email us directly. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

How do I purchase a floor model?

Browse products, select one you like, and click "Reserve" to hold it with an escrow payment. Schedule an on-site inspection at the showroom. If satisfied, accept the item and arrange pickup. Payment is released to the showroom upon acceptance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers. All payments are held in escrow until you inspect and accept the product at the showroom.

Can I inspect before buying?

Absolutely! On-site inspection is a required part of our process. You visit the showroom to inspect the product in person before finalizing the purchase. Only accept what meets your standards.

Who handles shipping?

All transactions are pickup-only. You arrange your own transportation and pickup directly from the showroom. This keeps costs low and ensures you see the product before finalizing.

What if the product doesn't match the listing?

If the product condition doesn't match the listing during your on-site inspection, you can reject it. Your escrowed payment will be refunded immediately. No questions asked.

Can I negotiate prices?

Prices are set by showrooms and displayed transparently on each listing. Some showrooms may be open to negotiation on high-value items. Contact them directly through the platform.

How do I list my showroom inventory?

Apply for a showroom account through our 30-day pilot program. Once approved, enter product SKUs and our AI tool automatically fills in specifications. Add photos, set your price, and publish. Listings go live immediately.

What commission do you charge?

Our standard commission is 2.5% on completed sales only. New showrooms joining our 30-day pilot program pay 0% commission for the first month. No upfront costs, no monthly fees, no hidden charges.

When do I get paid?

Buyer payments are held in escrow. Once the buyer inspects and accepts the product on-site, payment is released to you immediately via your preferred method (ACH, wire, or check).

Do I need to ship products?

No! All transactions are pickup-only at your showroom. Buyers handle their own transportation. You simply coordinate a convenient pickup time and release the product.

What if a buyer doesn't show up?

If a buyer reserves an item but doesn't show for inspection within the agreed timeframe, the reservation is cancelled and the item is automatically relisted. The buyer's payment is returned minus a small processing fee.

How long does it take to sell items?

Many floor models sell within days of listing. High-demand items from premium brands typically sell fastest. Our platform reaches thousands of verified trade buyers actively searching for inventory.

How does escrow protection work?

When a buyer reserves an item, their payment is held in a secure escrow account managed by our trusted financial partner. Funds are only released to the seller after the buyer inspects and accepts the product. This protects both parties.

What happens if there's a dispute?

Our support team mediates disputes fairly. We review listing details, photos, inspection notes, and communication between parties. Our goal is always a fair resolution that protects the integrity of the marketplace.

Are buyer identities verified?

Yes! All trade buyers must verify their business credentials and provide valid payment methods before making reservations. We screen for fraudulent activity and maintain a trusted buyer network.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never store complete payment information on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors.

Can I report suspicious activity?

Yes! If you encounter suspicious listings, communications, or behavior, report it immediately through our platform or contact form. We investigate all reports promptly and take action when necessary.
