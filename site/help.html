Most common issues

Missing parts

If a listing is incomplete, it must be disclosed. At pickup, buyers can decline if it doesn't match the disclosed completeness.

See condition & completeness rules

Damage mismatch

If the item is materially worse than listed (grade, photos, notes), the buyer can decline during inspection. No post-pickup disputes.
