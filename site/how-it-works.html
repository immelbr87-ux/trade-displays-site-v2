Purchasing Floor Models

Four simple steps to acquire premium kitchen & bath showroom inventory.

1

Browse & Reserve

Search premium floor models from verified showrooms. Reserve instantly with secure payment that's held in escrow until you accept the product.

2

Schedule Pickup

Coordinate a convenient pickup time directly with the showroom. Our platform facilitates communication and confirmation.

3

On-Site Inspection

Visit the showroom to inspect the product in person. Verify condition, dimensions, and specifications match the listing.

4

Accept & Pickup

If satisfied, accept the product and arrange pickup. Payment is released to the showroom upon your acceptance. All transactions are pickup only.
