End‑to‑end flow

Four steps. No scrolling maze.

1) List a Floor Model

Use the template: condition grade, photos, what’s included, known issues, and pickup window.

10–15 minutes

2) Buyers self‑select

Clear fields reduce “is this complete?” messages and filter out bad‑fit buyers early.

Fewer surprises

3) Confirm & coordinate

Confirm payment terms and pickup timing. Optional: buyer verification during pilot.

Simple coordination

4) Pickup & closeout

Buyer arrives in agreed window. Handoff occurs. Listing is marked complete.

Clean finish

Scope & expectations

A quick framing header above “What’s included / not included,” as requested.

What’s included

  • Showroom Floor Model listing template + consistent fields
  • Condition grading guidance and buyer-facing language
  • Pickup window structure (avoid “anytime this week” ambiguity)
  • Basic Trust & Brand Safety rules (see full page)

What’s not included (by default)

  • White-glove logistics or freight coordination
  • Warehousing, storage, or item refurbishment
  • Warranty creation (floor models are typically sold as‑is)
  • Price guarantees (pricing guidance can be provided during pilot)
