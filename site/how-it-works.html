The flow (end-to-end)

Simple, secure transactions from listing to pickup

1

List or Discover

Sellers create verified listings with detailed condition grading. Buyers browse in-stock inventory with transparent pricing.

2

Reserve with Escrow

Payment is held securely in escrow. Funds don't release until acceptance at pickup.

3

Schedule & Inspect

Coordinate pickup timing and location. Inspect in person to verify condition matches the listing.

4

Accept & Complete

Accept on-site, release funds, and arrange transport. No freight claims or post-pickup disputes.

Finality at pickup: No delivery, no returns after acceptance, and no post-pickup disputes. Once the buyer accepts on site, the sale is final and liability transfers to the buyer.

For Sellers (Showrooms)

Digitize pickup-only liquidation with cleaner protection and less admin

Why showrooms use this

  • The shipping headache: Heavy, unboxed floor models often require custom crating and expensive freight. Pickup-only removes the biggest logistics pain point.
  • Cash flow & space: List in 1 minute instead of running warehouse sales or email blasts.
  • Protection: Escrow protects sellers from post-pickup disputes. Once the buyer accepts at pickup, the deal is done.

What sellers get

  • Specs-first listings with clear condition grades
  • Scheduled pickup windows with dock notes
  • Fixed pricing (no haggling)
  • On-site acceptance → sales final after acceptance

For Buyers (Trade)

Get high-end items fast and under budget with pickup inspection

Why trade buyers use this

  • Instant gratification: Lead times for new high-end product can be 12–20+ weeks. Floor models are available now.
  • Risk mitigation: Inspect-at-pickup lets you reserve confidently and refuse only if the condition is materially worse than described.
  • Margin: Floor models can sell for 50–70% off, enabling savings for clients or healthier trade margin.

What's included

  • Pickup-only transactions (no shipping)
  • Buyer inspection before acceptance
  • Funds held until buyer acceptance
  • Clear condition standards and category templates
