Kohler compliance

A practical checklist to keep pilot listings clear, factual, and aligned with showroom operations.

Listing language

  • Use model/spec facts (finish, size, configuration). Avoid claims you can’t verify.
  • Describe condition using Grade A / Grade B and photos. No “like new” language.
  • State pickup-only and include an accurate pickup window and access notes.

Operational boundaries

  • Transactions are as-is. Buyer inspects on-site before acceptance.
  • No shipping coordination. No freight claims. Acceptance happens at pickup.
  • If parts are missing, disclose it up front (trim, valves, hardware, manuals).

Internal reporting

For pilots, we recommend tracking: time-to-reserve, pickup completion rate, and common mismatch categories.
