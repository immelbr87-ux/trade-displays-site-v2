Brand-Safe Pilot Overview

A simple internal review summary for brand-conscious showroom organizations.

Purpose

This page is designed to make a showroom pilot easy to review internally. It outlines what Trade Displays does and what it does not do.

What this pilot is

  • Pickup-only sales of showroom displays / clearance items
  • Clear condition disclosure and buyer inspection at pickup
  • Funds held until buyer confirms acceptance
  • Simple internal reporting on completed sales

What this pilot is not

  • No shipping or delivery coordination
  • No changes to manufacturer warranty policies
  • No public pricing mandates or advertised “discount programs”
  • No system integrations required

Brand-safe defaults

  • As-is language on every listing
  • Buyer inspection prior to acceptance
  • Disputes limited to material misrepresentation only
  • Listings can be deactivated at any time

Next step

To run a pilot, start with 2–3 items for one location. Review details on Pilot and submit the first listing via List a display.
