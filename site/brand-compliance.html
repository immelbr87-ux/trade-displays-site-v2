Brand Compliance Standards

Ensuring quality and authenticity in every listing.

Our Commitment to Quality

Trade Displays maintains strict brand compliance standards to protect buyers and preserve the reputation of display manufacturers and sellers.

Authentic Products Only

All displays must be authentic products from legitimate manufacturers. Counterfeit or knock-off displays are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension.

Accurate Branding

Listings must accurately represent the manufacturer, model, and specifications. Misrepresenting brands or claiming generic displays are name-brand products is prohibited.

Copyright & Trademark Respect

Sellers must respect all copyrights and trademarks. Display graphics and branding should be your own or properly licensed. Selling displays with copyrighted content without authorization is prohibited.

Manufacturer Guidelines

Sellers should follow any resale or rental restrictions set by manufacturers. Some brands have specific requirements for authorized resellers. Contact us if you have questions about specific brand policies.

Reporting Violations

If you see a listing that violates brand compliance standards, report it immediately to [email protected]. We investigate all reports within 24 hours.
