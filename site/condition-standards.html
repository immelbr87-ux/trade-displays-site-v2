Condition Standards

We keep condition grading intentionally simple to reduce disputes and speed up pickup. Showrooms list as Grade A or Grade B, and condition is confirmed at pickup.

Grades (Phase 1)

Grade A Light wear, fully functional. Minor cosmetic marks typical of showroom display.
Grade B Visible wear, fully functional. Cosmetic wear is noticeable but does not affect use.

What is always disclosed

  • Pickup window and location
  • Dimensions and finish family
  • Condition grade (A/B)
  • Any known cosmetic issues worth flagging (chips, scratches, discoloration)
Condition is confirmed at pickup The showroom confirms that the item matches the listed condition grade before escrow releases.
