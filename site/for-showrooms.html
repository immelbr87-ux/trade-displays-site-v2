Move floor models without the usual headache

Trade Displays gives your team a controlled, brand-safe way to sell showroom floor models with clear expectations: fixed pricing, upfront pickup windows, buyer inspection at pickup, and funds released only after acceptance.

Why showrooms use Trade Displays

  • Stale inventory → space pressure: floor models occupy premium floor space needed for new collections.
  • No shipping headache: pickup-only avoids custom crating, expensive freight, and damage claims.
  • Cash flow with less admin: list in under a minute instead of running warehouse sales or email blasts.
  • Protection at pickup: escrow + on-site acceptance reduces post-pickup disputes.

What we do (and don’t) do

Included

  • Buyer inspection + acceptance
  • Funds held until confirmation
  • Condition standards + disclosures
Not included
  • No delivery or shipping
  • No POS integrations
  • No warranty changes
  • No public pricing optics required
