Example listing format

This is an example of what a Trade Displays listing looks like. It’s designed for showroom inventory: clear specs, clear condition, pickup-only, and buyer confirmation at pickup.

Listing basics

  • Category: Bathroom vanity display
  • Size: 60" W × 22" D × 34" H (approx.)
  • Finish: White cabinet, matte black hardware
  • Included: Countertop (as installed)
  • Condition grade: Grade A

Condition notes

Minor cosmetic wear consistent with showroom use. No structural damage. Hardware included as shown. Buyers should inspect at pickup and confirm the listing matches before accepting.

Pickup + confirmation

Acceptance at pickup
