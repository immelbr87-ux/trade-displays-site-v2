Who Can Buy

Trade Displays is designed for trade buyers only. That keeps listings focused, reduces consumer traffic, and supports brand-safe resale of installed showroom displays.

Eligible buyer types

  • Licensed contractors and remodeling businesses
  • Home builders and flippers
  • Designers sourcing installs for clients
  • Property managers turning units

How verification works (Phase 1)

  • Provide business name + role (contractor / builder / designer / property manager).
  • Provide a business email or business website.
  • Optional: state license number (where applicable) or proof of trade account.
Not open to general consumers Listings are not marketed as retail clearance. This is a trade workflow with pickup confirmation and escrow controls.
