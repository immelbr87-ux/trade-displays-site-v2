Terms

Plain-English pilot terms for pickup-only transactions, buyer inspection, and as-is sales.

Summary (plain English)

  • Listings are sold as-is.
  • Transactions are pickup-only.
  • Buyers inspect at pickup and confirm acceptance before funds are released.
  • After buyer acceptance, the sale is final.
  • Disputes are limited to material misrepresentation.

Key terms

As-is sale. Items may have cosmetic wear, missing packaging, or display-related handling. Any included accessories or missing components must be disclosed in the listing.

Pickup & inspection. Buyers are expected to inspect before accepting. Sellers should provide reasonable access to view the item at pickup.

Acceptance. Buyer confirmation at pickup releases funds. Acceptance indicates the item matches the listing disclosures and condition grade.

Disputes. Eligible only for material misrepresentation (wrong model, undisclosed major damage, or missing critical components inconsistent with the listing). Cosmetic wear consistent with the grade is not eligible.

Transport

Buyers are responsible for transport and any required labor/equipment. Showrooms may provide basic loading assistance at their discretion, but are not required to pack, palletize, or ship items.

Limitation of liability

Trade Displays is a coordination and documentation layer for pickup transactions. We are not the manufacturer, we do not provide warranties, and we do not supervise installation or use. To the extent permitted by law, liability is limited to the amount paid in the transaction.
