Terms (Pilot Preview)

This is a plain-language summary for the pilot site. It is not legal advice and may be replaced by a signable agreement for participating showrooms.

Core rules

  • Pickup-only: No shipping, freight, or delivery is arranged through Trade Displays.
  • As-is: Floor models are sold as-is. Cosmetic wear is expected and disclosed via Grade A / Grade B.
  • Inspection at pickup: Buyer inspects on-site before accepting.
  • Escrow release: Funds release only after buyer acceptance at pickup.
  • No post-pickup disputes: Once accepted and removed from site, the transaction is final.

Mismatch handling

If the item materially differs from the listing (wrong model/specs, undisclosed damage beyond the stated grade, missing critical components disclosed as “included”), the buyer can decline acceptance on-site and escrow does not release. The seller may relist with corrected details.

Brand safety

Listings must be factual, specs-first, and avoid warranty or performance claims. Marketing language should not imply manufacturer endorsement.
