Plain-language summary (pilot)

Terms & Conditions

This page provides a clear summary of how Trade Displays works during the pilot. Installed showroom displays are sold as-is, with condition confirmed at pickup and escrow released only after confirmation.

Not legal advice This is a plain-language summary for pilot clarity. Pilot agreements govern participating showrooms.

Trade-only access

  • Accounts are intended for trade professionals (contractors, builders, designers sourcing installs, property managers).
  • Verification may be required before reserving.

Listings + brand-safe rules

  • Phase 1 listings are specs-first and avoid public brand names, SKUs, and model numbers.
  • Listings must not imply manufacturer warranty or “new-in-box” status.
  • Showrooms set fixed pricing and pickup windows.

Reservations + escrow

  • When a buyer reserves, funds are placed into escrow.
  • Escrow is released to the showroom only after pickup is confirmed.
  • If pickup does not occur, pilot policies define any applicable fees.

Condition + as-is sale

  • Displays are installed items sold as-is.
  • Condition is listed as Grade A or Grade B and confirmed at pickup.
  • No returns. The pickup confirmation step is the protection mechanism.

Pickup expectations

  • Buyer is responsible for pickup logistics during the stated window.
  • Showroom staff confirms condition at pickup using the stated grade.
