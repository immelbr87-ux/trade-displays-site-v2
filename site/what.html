What is Trade Displays?

A simple, brand-safe way for showrooms to list floor models for local pickup—without freight, crating, or endless back‑and‑forth.

For Sellers (Showrooms)

The “push”: why showrooms care

  • Stale inventory is expensive. Floor models take up premium space and slow new collection swaps.
  • Shipping is the headache. Heavy, unboxed items require custom crating and freight; damage claims are common. Pickup‑only removes the biggest operational pain.
  • Cash flow + space. Many showrooms rely on email blasts or warehouse sales. A “list in ~1 minute” flow is attractive if it consistently brings qualified buyers.
  • Protection at pickup. Escrow + buyer inspection at pickup reduces “it arrived damaged” disputes. Once accepted onsite, funds release.
Designed for brand safety: listings are clearly labeled as showroom floor models, sold as‑is, and kept pickup‑only.

For Buyers (Trade)

Builders, remodelers, designers, property managers

  • Fast local deals on quality fixtures and vanities—often same week.
  • Clear condition grades + photos + what’s included, so crews can plan confidently.
  • Simple pickup coordination with a defined pickup window and onsite inspection.
  • Escrow‑backed acceptance: confirm condition at pickup, then the transaction is finalized.
Important: Bring the right vehicle + help. Items are typically heavy and unboxed.
