HOW IT WORKS

Verified pickup, logistics-first.

Trade Displays is built for high-trust physical assets: showroom floor models that are heavy, unboxed, and time-sensitive. The entire system is designed around inspection, clean releases, and pickup execution.

1 • LIST

List a floor model

Specs-first listing. Grade A or Grade B. Photos. Pickup window. “Ready for transit” if it’s de-installed and staged.

2 • RESERVE

Buyer reserves

Buyer requests the item and confirms pickup readiness. Payment is held in escrow—no pressure on the showroom.

3 • INSPECT

Inspect on-site

At pickup, buyer checks grade + completeness against the listing and photos. If it matches, they accept.

4 • RELEASE

Confirm & release

Buyer confirms acceptance. Escrow releases. After acceptance, it’s final—no post-pickup disputes or freight claims.

What’s included

  • Pickup-only workflow (no shipping/freight)
  • Condition grades (A/B) + completeness notes
  • Buyer inspection at pickup + acceptance confirmation
  • Escrow held until acceptance

What’s not included

  • De-install or removal labor
  • Freight coordination, crating, or shipping claims
  • Post-pickup returns or disputes
