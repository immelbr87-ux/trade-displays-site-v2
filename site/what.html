What this is (and what it is not)

Trade Displays lets showrooms pre-sell scheduled floor-model removals to verified trade buyers using fixed pricing, pickup windows, and escrow until pickup confirmation.

Browse upcoming displays Request pilot access Brand compliance

What this is

Inventory
Scheduled removals

Listings exist only when a showroom has a real removal window.

Structure
Fixed price + pickup window

No bidding. No negotiation threads. The pickup window is enforced.

Payment
Escrow until pickup

Funds release only after pickup confirmation.

What this is not

Not a consumer marketplace

  • No open-ended consumer browsing “for everything”
  • No consumer-to-showroom DMs or negotiation
  • No public marketing framing that implies brand blowouts

Not retail “new” promises

  • No manufacturer warranty language
  • No “new” status claims
  • Final sale — designed around pickup inspection + confirmation
Why these boundaries matter They keep the program brand-safe, operationally clean for showrooms, and focused on trade buyers who already purchase displays and open-box inventory.
Trade Displays is operated by Bargain Bond. Escrow and commitment are facilitated for scheduled display removals.
