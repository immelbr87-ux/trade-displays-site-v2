1. Introduction

Welcome to THE SHOWROOM MARKET, operated by Bargain Bond. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information you provide when you:

  • Create an account (name, email, phone, business information)
  • List products (showroom location, business credentials)
  • Make purchases (billing information, pickup location)
  • Contact us (inquiry details, messages)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (IP address, browser type)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, interactions)
  • Location data (general geographic location)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our marketplace services
  • Process transactions and manage escrow payments
  • Verify user identity and business credentials
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
  • Improve our Platform and user experience
  • Send important notices and updates
  • Prevent fraud and ensure Platform security

4. Information Sharing

We share your information only when:

  • Necessary to complete transactions (e.g., sharing pickup location with buyers)
  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect rights, property, or safety
  • With service providers who assist our operations (payment processors, hosting)
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
