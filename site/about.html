What it is

A specs-first marketplace for local pickup of showroom floor models. Buyers inspect at pickup; funds are held until acceptance.

Why it exists

Shipping heavy, unboxed floor models is costly and claim-prone. Local pickup removes the biggest operational headache while preserving a clean release workflow.

What we optimize for

  • Logistics-first: pickup windows, readiness signals, and coordination clarity.
  • Verification: signals like Verified Pickup and condition grading (cosmetic-only).
  • Brand safety: specs-first listings reduce exposure while still enabling confident buying.
