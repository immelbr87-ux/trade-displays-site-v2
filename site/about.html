About Trade Displays

We're building the future of trade show display marketplaces.

Our Mission

Trade Displays exists to make the buying, selling, and renting of trade show displays simple, transparent, and trustworthy. We believe exhibitors deserve a marketplace that understands their unique needs and provides professional, reliable service.

Our Story

Founded by trade show industry veterans, Trade Displays was born from a simple observation: there was no dedicated marketplace for trade show displays.

Exhibitors were forced to navigate fragmented marketplaces, unclear pricing, and unreliable sellers. We knew there had to be a better way.

Today, we're building a platform that brings transparency, trust, and professionalism to an industry that deserves it. Our marketplace connects verified sellers with serious buyers, ensuring every transaction is secure and straightforward.
