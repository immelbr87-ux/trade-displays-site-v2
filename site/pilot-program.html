Pilot Program

The pilot is designed to be low-friction for showrooms and predictable for trade buyers. Start with a handful of known removals and validate the workflow end-to-end.

What the pilot includes

  • Showroom listing flow (specs + A/B condition + pickup window)
  • Trade buyer verification
  • Reservation confirmation + escrow hold
  • Pickup confirmation step (staff quick check)

Minimum requirements

  • Known removal date or removal window
  • Pickup window defined in advance
  • Condition listed as Grade A or Grade B
  • Brand-safe listing standards followed
