A small, reversible pilot that proves the workflow

The pilot is intentionally narrow: pickup-only, buyer inspection before acceptance, and clean reporting. If it doesn’t add value or creates friction, we shut it off.

Pilot scope

  • Locations: 1 showroom
  • Inventory: 2–5 items (floor models / clearance)
  • Fulfillment: pickup-only
  • Duration: 30 days

No integrations. No minimums. No long-term commitment.

What stays out of scope

  • No delivery or shipping
  • No returns after acceptance
  • No changes to warranties or brand policies
  • No requirement to show brand in listing titles (pilot option)

Success criteria

  • At least one completed pickup (buyer accepted; funds released)
  • No disputes or only clearly-resolved edge cases
  • Staff time to list feels manageable between customers
  • Sales summary is usable for leadership reporting

Exit is simple

  • Deactivate listings at any time
  • No obligation to continue
  • We provide a final recap of completed sales

How pricing works after a successful pilot

If the pilot is successful, the cleanest structure for most showrooms is a simple monthly fee per location (no listing fees, no commission). We keep it month‑to‑month so it remains reversible.

We do not ask for payment details to start the pilot.
