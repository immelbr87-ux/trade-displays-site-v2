Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to join?

You must be an established kitchen & bath showroom with floor models to list, valid business credentials, and the ability to facilitate on-site pickups.

How long does approval take?

We review all applications within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email with your account activation details and next steps.

What happens after the 30-day pilot?

After the 30-day pilot, you'll transition to our standard 2.5% commission rate. Pilot members receive priority support and ongoing benefits.

How do pickups work?

All transactions are pickup only at your showroom. Buyers coordinate pickup times directly with you, inspect the product on-site, and arrange their own transportation.

How do payments work?

Buyers pay upfront through our secure escrow system. Funds are held until the buyer accepts the product at your showroom, then released to your account within 2-3 business days.
