This pilot is designed to monetize floor models without public markdown optics, consumer negotiation, or last-minute scrambling. You list upcoming removals with a pickup window and condition grade. A verified trade buyer reserves. Funds are held until pickup confirmation.

Key point You’re not running a “floor model blowout.” You’re scheduling a professional pickup of an installed showroom display that’s already planned for removal.

What we need from you (minimum)

  • Confirm the item is an installed showroom display and is scheduled for removal.
  • Set a fixed price, a pickup window, and a condition grade (A or B).
  • Provide specs-first listing details (dimensions, rough-in, finish family, functional completeness).
  • Agree to pickup confirmation (QR + staff confirmation) to release escrow.
