No integrations. No minimums. No long-term commitment.

What stays out of scope

  • No delivery or shipping

Success criteria

  • At least one completed pickup (buyer accepted; funds released)
  • No disputes or only clearly-resolved edge cases
  • Staff time to list feels manageable between customers
  • Sales summary is usable for leadership reporting

Exit Is Simple

  • Deactivate listings at any time
  • No obligation to continue
  • We provide a final recap of completed sales

Commission & Fees (the “Success Fee”)

We only get paid when you actually clear items off your floor. Commission is deducted automatically from escrow prior to payout to the showroom.

  • 15% for items sold under $5,000
  • 10% for items sold between $5,001 – $20,000
  • 7% for items sold over $20,001

We do not ask for payment details to start the pilot.
