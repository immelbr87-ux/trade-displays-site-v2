Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to join?

You must have at least one trade show display to list, valid business credentials, and the ability to ship displays to buyers.

How long does approval take?

We review all applications within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email with next steps.

What happens after the pilot program?

Pilot members will transition to our standard plan with a permanently reduced commission rate as a thank you for early support.

Can I list displays for both rent and sale?

Absolutely! You can list the same display for both rental and purchase, or separate listings for different inventory.

How do payments work?

We hold payment securely until the buyer confirms receipt. Funds are then transferred to your account within 2-3 business days.
